Your Mission

You are a next-generation tank complete with JUMPING ABILITIES, a SHIELD, and THREE types of weapons. You have been dropped off at the LANDING ZONE and must infiltrate Scene_1 — the lair of the criminal BOSS TURRET — and destroy it.

Be careful! Along the way, you will encounter WANDERING ENEMIES and SMALLER TURRETS.

Controls

  W               move forward
  A               move left
  D               move right
  S               move back
  
  Q E             cycle weapon
  SPACE           jump (with a tank?!)
  F               toggle mouse capture
  
  Mouse look      direction
  
  Left Click      shoot
  Right Click     enable shield
  
  ;               toggle full screen
  "               toggle first-person view

Debug Controls

  P O             increase / decrease normal map depth
  L K             enable / disable fog layer
